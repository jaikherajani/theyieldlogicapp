# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 20 March 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data YieldLogic collects, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 1. Who We Are

YieldLogic (also referred to as "The Yield Logic App", "The Yield Logic", "Yield Logic", or "theyieldlogic" in various contexts) is an independently developed iOS application for personal property investment analysis. The developer is an individual, not a registered company or corporation.

Contact: [jai.kherajani19@icloud.com](mailto:jai.kherajani19@icloud.com)

---

## 2. Data We Collect

YieldLogic collects the minimum data necessary to function.

**Data you provide:**
- Property names, addresses, and financial figures you enter into the app
- Scenario names, income rates, expense figures, and mortgage details
- Your preferred market and app settings

**Data collected automatically via Sign In with Apple:**
- An anonymous Apple User ID (a stable identifier provided by Apple)
- Your name and email address, only if you choose to share them with Apple's consent prompt

**We do not collect:**
- Location data
- Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
- Any advertising identifiers
- Crash reports or analytics sent to any third-party service (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel)

**Apple's role in analytics:**
If you have opted in to share app analytics with developers in your iOS Settings, Apple may provide us with anonymous, aggregated metrics such as crash reports and session counts through App Store Connect. This data is fully anonymised and controlled by Apple — we have no ability to identify individual users from it.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Stored

All property analyses, scenarios, and financial data you create are stored:

- Locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- In your private iCloud account (if iCloud sync is enabled on your device), using Apple's CloudKit framework

The developer does not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure of their own. Your financial data never leaves the Apple ecosystem.

Your Sign In with Apple credentials are stored securely in your device's Keychain. The developer cannot access your Keychain.

---

## 4. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to:
- Display your property analysis and scenarios within the app
- Perform financial calculations on your device
- Sync your data across your own Apple devices via iCloud

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third party.

The only third-party service involved is Apple Inc., which provides:
- Sign In with Apple (authentication)
- iCloud / CloudKit (optional sync)

Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy: [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

Pro subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive, store, or process your payment card details. Receipt validation is handled on-device via StoreKit.

## 7. Children's Privacy

YieldLogic is intended for adults aged **18 and over**. The app involves real estate financial analysis, mortgage calculations, and paid subscriptions — content and functionality not appropriate for children. We do not knowingly permit use of the app by anyone under 18.

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our own servers. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device and in your own private iCloud account, which is fully under your control. There is nothing held by the developer that could be deleted on your behalf.

---

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device and iCloud account for as long as you use the app or until you delete it.

**To delete individual items:**
- Delete properties or scenarios directly within the app.

**To delete all data and close your account:**
- Go to Settings → Account → "Delete Account & Data". This permanently removes all properties, scenarios, and settings from your device and iCloud. This cannot be undone.

**To remove iCloud data manually (if you have already uninstalled the app):**
- Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → YieldLogic → Delete Data.

**To revoke Sign In with Apple access:**
- Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Password & Security → Sign in with Apple → YieldLogic → Stop Using Apple ID.

**If any data appears to remain after following the steps above:**

In rare cases, iCloud may retain file caches or metadata briefly after deletion. If you believe residual data is still present after uninstalling the app and completing the steps above, you can:

1. Visit [icloud.com](https://www.icloud.com), sign in, and check "iCloud Drive" for any YieldLogic folder — delete it if present.
2. Contact Apple Support directly at [apple.com/support](https://www.apple.com/support/) to request removal of any remaining data from iCloud infrastructure associated with this app's private container. Apple controls the underlying iCloud storage and can assist with complete removal upon request.

The developer does not have access to your iCloud container and cannot perform this removal on your behalf.

## 9. Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device and in your own iCloud account, you already have full and direct control over it at all times. The developer does not hold a copy of your data on any server and therefore cannot access, modify, or delete it on your behalf.

- **To access your data:** it is available directly within the app.
- **To correct your data:** edit or delete entries directly in the app.
- **To delete your data:** see Section 8 above.
- **To export your data:** in-app export is not currently supported. Your data is stored in your private iCloud account and is accessible through Apple's iCloud platform.

If you have a question about your data that is not answered here, you may contact us (see Section 12), though our ability to assist is limited given we hold no copy of your data.

---

## 10. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

- All data is stored in Apple's managed frameworks (SwiftData, CloudKit, Keychain), which use industry-standard encryption
- iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Keychain entries are encrypted by the device's secure enclave

No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
